A small autobiography

My name is Ruslan. I live in Kiev and study at the 2nd year of the State University of Telecommunications, speciality - "software engineering". I grew up in a small town, not far from Vinnitsa, after finishing school I entered in the university and moved to the capital. Already in the first year I realized in which direction I would like to develop and work: I dream of becoming a Web developer and I make every efforts to do this. Because the Internet is an interesting, multifaced and not fully understood thing. Almost every day there are some new items that you want to learn, check with your own hand and in time get some benefit from this.
